# Third-Party Notices

This file tracks third-party materials that are not intentionally relicensed as
project-owned code.

Dependencies are licensed by their respective authors and maintainers. Their
license metadata is managed through the package manager and upstream package
registries.

The project license does not replace or relicense dependency licenses.

## Names and trademarks

Product names, company names, supplier names, service names, and trademarks
referenced in the repository remain the property of their respective owners.
Their presence in documentation, code, tests, screenshots, or fixtures does not
grant ownership or trademark rights.

## Assets and copied materials

No third-party assets or copied third-party source code are intentionally
relicensed as project-owned code.

If future work adds third-party code, assets, datasets, fixtures, media, or
other materials that require attribution, add them to this file with:

- source;
- original author or owner;
- license;
- attribution requirements;
- usage location in this repository;
- any redistribution or modification limits.

## Review expectation

Before adding third-party materials, confirm that the license is compatible with
the repository's `GPL-3.0-only` licensing posture and that any attribution or
notice requirement is documented here.
